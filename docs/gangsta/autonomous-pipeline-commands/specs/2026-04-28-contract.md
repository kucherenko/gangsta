# Contract: Autonomous Pipeline Commands

## Objective

Add an opt-in autonomous shortcut to the Gangsta framework: `/gangsta:heist <feature>` runs the Plan stage (Reconnaissance → Grilling → Sit-Down → Resource Development) end-to-end without requiring per-gate Don approval, and `/gangsta:go [feature]` runs the Execution stage (The Hit → Laundering). A new `/gangsta:abort <feature>` command preserves Don repudiation rights. The autonomous mode is governed by two new skills (`gangsta:autonomous-mode`, `gangsta:don-proxy`) that delegate per-gate Don decisions to a surrogate authority bounded by a non-negotiable Constitutional Floor, with the real Don retaining terminal authority via `/go` (sign), `/abort` (reject), and the Consigliere's existing terminal veto in the Sit-Down. The gated Heist remains the default and operates unchanged.

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

**Skills**
- FR-001 A new skill `gangsta:autonomous-mode` SHALL exist at `skills/autonomous-mode/SKILL.md` and define the orchestration of `/heist` and `/go`.
- FR-002 `gangsta:autonomous-mode` SHALL contain a section "Per-Phase Interaction Schemas" with one subsection per host skill (Reconnaissance, the-Grilling, the-Sit-Down, Resource-Development, the-Hit, Laundering). Each subsection specifies how `gangsta:don-proxy` is invoked, what artifacts are produced, what triggers REJECT, and which existing skill rules are preserved unchanged.
- FR-003 `gangsta:autonomous-mode` SHALL specify three persistent state files: `docs/gangsta/.last-heist` (single line: absolute path to most recent autonomous heist directory), `docs/gangsta/<feature>/autonomous-log.md` (progressive per-phase decision log written during `/heist`), and `docs/gangsta/<feature>/pressure-test-plan.md` (baseline + change scenarios per AGENTS.md:18-21).
- FR-004 A new skill `gangsta:don-proxy` SHALL exist at `skills/don-proxy/SKILL.md` and define the surrogate Don authority for autonomous gates. It SHALL be a distinct skill from `gangsta:the-consigliere`.
- FR-005 `gangsta:don-proxy` SHALL contain a section titled "Constitutional Floor (Non-Negotiable)" stating that the don-proxy MUST reject any artifact (Dossier, Grilling consensus, Contract, Plan, Hit deliverable, Laundering verdict) violating any Omerta law in `skills/omerta/SKILL.md` or any active Negative Constraint in `docs/gangsta/constitution.md`. The Constitutional Floor SHALL apply to don-proxy decisions in **all six phases** (universal scope; not limited to Sit-Down).
- FR-006 `gangsta:don-proxy` SHALL specify a "boldness" persona (prefers decisive action and strategic risk-taking) operative only above the Constitutional Floor. The persona text SHALL NOT instruct the don-proxy to override Omerta or Negative Constraints.

**Sit-Down Dual-Veto Protocol**
- FR-007 In autonomous mode, the Sit-Down SHALL require non-REJECT verdicts from BOTH `gangsta:the-consigliere` (spec integrity) and `gangsta:don-proxy` (boldness/strategic fit + Constitutional Floor). Either REJECT verdict is terminal: the heist SHALL be aborted via the `/gangsta:abort` mechanism (FR-019) without further phases.
- FR-008 The auto-advance at `skills/the-sit-down/SKILL.md:164` SHALL fire in autonomous mode when don-proxy signs the Contract AND Consigliere has not REJECTed. The auto-advance SHALL NOT fire if either authority REJECTs.

**Hit Phase Constraint**
- FR-009 In autonomous mode, the mini-Grilling escalation at `skills/the-hit/SKILL.md:102` SHALL produce a deviation report only and SHALL NOT mutate the signed Contract. Any required Contract amendment SHALL trigger a return to a fresh Sit-Down (with fresh Consigliere review).

**Carve-outs**
- FR-010 The following six skills SHALL each contain a single carve-out reference to `gangsta:autonomous-mode`'s Per-Phase Interaction Schemas: `skills/reconnaissance/SKILL.md`, `skills/the-grilling/SKILL.md`, `skills/the-sit-down/SKILL.md` (referenced near both line 154 Consigliere review and line 164 auto-advance), `skills/resource-development/SKILL.md`, `skills/the-hit/SKILL.md` (referenced near line 102 mini-Grilling), `skills/laundering/SKILL.md`. The carve-out text in each skill SHALL be substantively the same one-line reference of the form: "**Autonomous Mode:** When invoked under `gangsta:autonomous-mode`, see § Per-Phase Interaction Schemas → \<Phase\> in that skill. Otherwise this skill operates as written."
- FR-011 No carve-out SHALL contain implementation details that could drift from `gangsta:autonomous-mode`. All per-phase autonomous-mode behavior SHALL be defined exclusively in `gangsta:autonomous-mode`.

**Amendment to using-gangsta**
- FR-012 `skills/using-gangsta/SKILL.md:144` ("Never auto-advance") SHALL be amended to (a) preserve the two existing auto-advances (`the-sit-down:164` and `the-hit:110`) explicitly, and (b) permit auto-advance under `gangsta:autonomous-mode` where Don approval is delegated to `gangsta:don-proxy` and the real Don's terminal authority is retained via `/gangsta:go` (sign), `/gangsta:abort` (reject), and the Consigliere's existing terminal veto.

**Constitution Initialization**
- FR-013 A new file `docs/gangsta/constitution.md` SHALL exist with sections for Commandments and Negative Constraints, a cross-reference to `skills/omerta/SKILL.md` declaring Omerta laws as binding, and at minimum **one** seed Negative Constraint specific to autonomous mode (the seed: "NEVER weaken or bypass Omerta Law 2 (checkpoints non-negotiable) in any autonomous-mode pathway — Source: this Heist's Recon Dossier"). Additional Commandments and Negative Constraints MAY be added by future Heists' Laundering phase.

**Commands**
- FR-014 A new command `/gangsta:heist` SHALL exist with required positional argument `<feature>` and CLI flags `--retries=N` (integer 1–10, default 3), `--rounds=N` (integer 1–7, default 3 — range matches `skills/the-grilling/SKILL.md` ceiling), `--best-effort=true|false` (default true).
- FR-015 `/gangsta:heist` SHALL run Reconnaissance → Grilling → Sit-Down → Resource-Development autonomously with `gangsta:don-proxy` as the per-gate decision authority. On successful completion it SHALL write `docs/gangsta/.last-heist` (containing the absolute path to the heist directory) and emit an inline chat summary listing all produced artifact paths and a per-phase decision log reference.
- FR-016 `/gangsta:heist` SHALL abort (no Hit, no Laundering) on don-proxy REJECT or Consigliere REJECT, leaving the heist directory in place for Don inspection. (`/gangsta:abort` is a separate explicit command for Don repudiation.) A don-proxy REJECT in **any** phase (Reconnaissance, Grilling, Sit-Down, Resource-Development, the-Hit, or Laundering) under the Constitutional Floor SHALL be terminal: `/gangsta:heist` SHALL stop the heist immediately, write the REJECT verdict and citation to `autonomous-log.md`, and leave the heist directory in place for Don inspection. Resumption requires a fresh `/gangsta:heist` invocation; mid-flight retry of a Constitutional-Floor REJECT is not permitted regardless of `--best-effort` setting.
- FR-017 A new command `/gangsta:go` SHALL exist with optional positional argument `[feature]` and CLI flags `--retries=N`, `--best-effort=true|false`. If `<feature>` is provided, `docs/gangsta/<feature>/` MUST exist exactly (no fuzzy match, no Levenshtein); mismatch SHALL produce a hard error with no signing. If `<feature>` is omitted, `/gangsta:go` SHALL read `docs/gangsta/.last-heist` and use that path; missing or stale pointer SHALL produce a hard error.
- FR-018 `/gangsta:go` SHALL be the real-Don signing event. At start, it SHALL flip Contract status frontmatter from `signed-by: don-proxy, status: pending-don-confirmation` to `signed-by: don, confirmed: <ISO-8601>` and apply equivalent transitions to all ledger entries whose frontmatter `heist:` key matches `<feature>` and whose `status:` key is `pending-don-confirmation`. It SHALL then run The Hit → Laundering autonomously under `gangsta:autonomous-mode`.
- FR-019 A new command `/gangsta:abort` SHALL exist with required positional argument `<feature>`. It SHALL move `docs/gangsta/<feature>/` to `docs/gangsta/.aborted/<feature>-<ISO-8601>/` and write `abort-marker.md` at the new location with frontmatter `signed-by: don`, `rejected: <ISO-8601>`, `original-path: docs/gangsta/<feature>`. It SHALL clear `docs/gangsta/.last-heist` if that pointer references the aborted feature.
- FR-020 Reads of the active ledger (`docs/gangsta/insights/`, `docs/gangsta/fails/`) by `gangsta:reconnaissance` and any other consumer SHALL exclude entries under `docs/gangsta/.aborted/`.

**Plugin and Validation**

If registration of `cfg.command.paths` fails or the key is not honored by the running OpenCode version, the plugin SHALL fall back to directory-based discovery of `commands/` such that the three commands remain invocable. The fallback behavior SHALL be documented in a one-line comment in `.opencode/plugins/gangsta.js` adjacent to the registration site.

- FR-023 `scripts/validate.sh` SHALL be extended to (a) verify each `commands/*.md` file has valid YAML frontmatter with required keys, (b) verify the three command files (`heist.md`, `go.md`, `abort.md`) all exist, and (c) verify the two new skills exist with non-empty SKILL.md files.

**Ledger Frontmatter Protocol**
- FR-024 Ledger entries written by `gangsta:laundering` during an autonomous heist SHALL include frontmatter `signed-by: don-proxy`, `status: pending-don-confirmation`, `heist: <feature>`, `date: <ISO-8601>`. `/gangsta:go` SHALL update these to `signed-by: don`, `confirmed: <ISO-8601>` (preserving `heist:` and `date:`). `/gangsta:abort` SHALL relocate them with the heist directory.

**Out-of-Scope Documentation**

## Architectural Decisions

### AD-001 Two new skills, not one (and not amendments)
`gangsta:autonomous-mode` (orchestration) and `gangsta:don-proxy` (decision authority) are separate skills. Rationale: orchestration is mechanism, decision authority is policy; conflating them couples scheduling logic to chain-of-command semantics. The existing Consigliere is NOT amended to play the Don role — adding Don-proxy duties to a skill whose contract is "spec-integrity auditor" would create an authority conflict (decided in Grilling Round 1 Q5).

### AD-002 Centralized per-phase schemas, single-line carve-outs
All per-phase autonomous-mode behavior lives in `gangsta:autonomous-mode` § Per-Phase Interaction Schemas. The six host skills receive substantively-identical one-line references. Rationale: prevents drift across six dispatch sites as host skills evolve (decided in Grilling Round 2 Fix #5).

### AD-003 Sit-Down Dual-Veto, Either-REJECT-Terminal
Both Consigliere and don-proxy must non-REJECT in autonomous Sit-Down. Either REJECT is terminal — the two vetoes are symmetric, with no precedence between them. Rationale: symmetric termination resolves the deadlock that would otherwise arise from two outside-chain-of-command authorities with opposing biases (boldness vs spec-integrity); precedence is unnecessary because either authority firing REJECT immediately ends the heist (decided in Grilling Round 2 Fix #1).

### AD-004 `/go` as signing event with `.last-heist` pointer + exact match only
No fuzzy match, no Levenshtein. `/heist` writes `.last-heist`; `/go` without args reads it; `/go <name>` requires exact directory match. Rationale: fuzzy matching over a growing `docs/gangsta/` directory creates a TOCTOU forged-signing pathway where a typo can promote the wrong Contract to `don-confirmed`. The pointer + exact-match design eliminates the attack surface entirely (decided in Grilling Round 2 Fix #2).

### AD-005 Universal Constitutional Floor on don-proxy
The Constitutional Floor (mandatory rejection of Omerta-violating or Negative-Constraint-violating artifacts) applies to don-proxy decisions in **all six phases**, not only Sit-Down. Rationale: Recon and Resource-Development decisions can introduce constitutional violations earlier than Sit-Down; the floor must operate everywhere don-proxy has authority (decided in Grilling Round 2 Q2).

### AD-006 Explicit reject path via `/gangsta:abort`
The Don is not required to "walk away" to repudiate a don-proxy-signed Contract. `/gangsta:abort <feature>` relocates the directory to `.aborted/` with a Don-signed marker. Rationale: walking away pollutes the active directory and ledger, corrupting Omerta L2 audit posture. Explicit abort preserves the audit trail (decided in Grilling Round 2 Fix #4).

### AD-007 Mandatory Grilling, no `--no-grilling` flag
The autonomous Plan stage cannot skip the Grilling. Rationale: skipping adversarial review is exactly the failure mode autonomous mode is most likely to be misused for; the `--rounds=1` floor provides a fast path while preserving the discipline (decided in Grilling Round 1 Q2 + Round 2 Fix #7).

### AD-008 Two-PR delivery: skills + commands
PR1 = skills, carve-outs, amendment, Constitution init, pressure-test plan. PR2 = commands, plugin extension, validate.sh extension. The `/gangsta:purge-aborted` command is explicitly deferred to a post-PR2 PR. Rationale: each PR has a single concern and is independently shippable; the bulk-PR rule in `AGENTS.md:12` is respected (decided in Grilling Round 1 Q1 evolution + Round 2 PR1/PR2 retention).

### AD-009 Mini-Grilling cannot mutate Contract
Under autonomous mode, `the-hit:102` mini-Grilling produces a deviation report only. Contract amendments require a fresh Sit-Down with fresh Consigliere review. Rationale: closes the backdoor whereby don-proxy could revise a signed Contract mid-Hit without Consigliere oversight (decided in Grilling Round 2 finding #10).

## Grilling Conclusions

### Key Decisions
- Two-skill architecture (autonomous-mode + don-proxy) — see AD-001.
- Centralized per-phase schemas with one-line carve-outs — see AD-002.
- Sit-Down Dual-Veto with either-REJECT-terminal (symmetric, no precedence) — see AD-003.
- `.last-heist` pointer + exact-match `/go`, fuzzy match removed entirely — see AD-004.
- Universal Constitutional Floor across all phases — see AD-005.
- `/gangsta:abort` as explicit reject path — see AD-006.
- Mandatory Grilling, `--no-grilling` flag dropped — see AD-007.
- Two-PR delivery, `/gangsta:purge-aborted` deferred post-PR2 — see AD-008.
- Mini-Grilling cannot mutate Contract — see AD-009.
- Real ledger with `signed-by: don-proxy, status: pending-don-confirmation` frontmatter; `/go` flips, `/abort` relocates. No `proposed-ledger/` two-tier (decided Grilling Round 1 Q4).

### Rejected Alternatives
- **Amend `gangsta:the-consigliere` directly with a Don-Proxy Mode section** — rejected. Conflates auditor and decision authority into one skill; creates outside-chain authority conflict. Replaced by separate `gangsta:don-proxy` skill (Grilling Round 1 Q5).
- **Three-command UX (`/heist`, `/approve`, `/go`)** — rejected. The `/approve` step adds friction without value once `/go` itself signs the Contract (Grilling Round 1 Q7).
- **Forged signature `signatories: [autonomous-mode]`** — rejected. Forged signatures violate Omerta truth posture. Replaced by real `signed-by: don-proxy` proxy signature (Grilling Round 1 Q3).
- **`proposed-ledger/` two-tier directory structure** — rejected. Adds complexity without value; the real ledger with `pending-don-confirmation` frontmatter and `/abort` relocation achieves the same audit isolation (Grilling Round 1 Q4).
- **`--no-grilling` CLI flag** — rejected. Skipping adversarial review is the highest-risk misuse path (Grilling Round 1 Q2).
- **Fuzzy-match `/go` (Levenshtein ≤ 2 + recency tiebreaker)** — rejected. Forged-signing TOCTOU pathway. Replaced by `.last-heist` pointer + exact-match (Grilling Round 2 Fix #2).
- **Constitutional Floor scoped only to Sit-Down** — rejected. Constraints can be violated in earlier phases too (Grilling Round 2 Q2).
- **Concurrent `/heist` lock file in PR2** — rejected. Out-of-scope for this Heist; future PR if observed in practice (Grilling Round 2 Q3).
- **Auto-prune `.aborted/` after N days** — rejected. Indefinite retention preferred for audit posture; future `/gangsta:purge-aborted` command if needed (Grilling Round 2 Q4).

### Unresolved Objections
- **Mandatory Grilling token cost vs autonomous-speed claim** — Risk: MEDIUM — Mitigation: `--rounds=1` floor provides minimum-cost fast path; speed claim restated as "structured fast path" in `gangsta:autonomous-mode`. Acceptable.
- **End-of-`/heist` review as a single rubber-stamp gate** — Risk: MEDIUM — Mitigation: progressive `autonomous-log.md` lets the Don monitor decisions as they happen, and `/gangsta:abort` provides a clean reject path post-summary. Acceptable.

## Open Risks

- **Risk-001 [MEDIUM]: Token cost of mandatory Grilling under `/heist`.** A default `--rounds=3` autonomous heist dispatches multiple subagents per round; total token spend may exceed gated mode for small heists. Mitigation: `--rounds=1` floor; speed claim positioned as "structured fast path" in `gangsta:autonomous-mode`. Accepted.
- **Risk-002 [MEDIUM]: Single end-of-`/heist` review as a consolidated gate.** A Don who approves four files at once may rubber-stamp where they would have caught issues incrementally. Mitigation: progressive `autonomous-log.md` lets the Don monitor mid-flight; `/gangsta:abort` provides a clean reject path without manual filesystem edits. Accepted.
- **Risk-003 [LOW]: Constitutional Floor enforcement depends on don-proxy honoring the rule.** No external mechanism prevents a future skill edit from weakening the floor. Mitigation: AC-016 pressure-test exercises the floor; future skill changes that weaken it will fail the test in its PR's pressure-test results.
